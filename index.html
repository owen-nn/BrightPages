What is BrightPages?

BrightPages shines a beacon of hope and enlightenment in the heart of correctional facilities. As a federally incorporated non-profit organization, we are on a mission to transform the lives of those within the prison system through the power of reading and education. With a robust network that spans schools and bookshops, we collect a diverse range of books and channel them towards prisons across the nation. Our work doesn't stop at donation; we delve deeper, establishing comprehensive library systems within correctional facilities to ensure that every inmate has access to a world of knowledge, inspiration, and hope right at their fingertips. By reinforcing existing libraries with an abundance of books and resources, we empower vulnerable individuals to embark on a journey of self-improvement and learning. BrightPages is more than just a non-profit; we are a movement towards creating a more inclusive, educated, and compassionate society. Join us in turning the page towards a brighter future for all..
